Balyakalasakhi

Book Name : Balyakalasakhi
Author : Vaikom Muhammad Basheer
Category : Fiction,Romance
Language : Malayalam
Publishing Date : 1 january 2019

The book explores themes such as the differences between the worlds of boys and girls, how poverty can destroy dreams, the impact of death on life, and the ups and downs of life's standard of living. Vaikom Muhammad Basheer was a Malayalam writer, humanist, and independence activist. He was known for his down-to-earth style of writing that was popular with both literary critics and the general public.
